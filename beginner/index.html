Beginner Abacus Fundamentals Course

This is the first of three courses that aim to teach multi-digit number calculations. The beginner course gives students a chance to practice reading numbers on the abacus while carrying out the most simple direct movements. It begins with direct-4 questions that only involve the bottom four beads and progresses to direct-9 questions that use bottom and top beads.
