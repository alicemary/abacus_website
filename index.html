Varying Difficulty

Abacus Fundamentals Course

This leveled system provides worksheets for students working toward summing any three-digit numbers on the abacus. It covers bead values, the small-friend concept and the borrow and carry techniques. Each level should be practiced repeatedly before moving on as these skills are essential to the more advanced mathematics that can be performed on the abacus.

Beginner

Get acquainted with abacus bead values and try out some basic math.

Intermediate

Build strong foundations with multi-digit small-friend calculations.

Advanced

Master multi-digit numbers on the abacus with borrow and carry.
